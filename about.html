About me: In a snippet

I am currently a Master student at Carnegie Mellon University. My inspiration to getting involved with the Human Computer Interaction field is to be a person who is capable of transmitting information while playing a role in elevating human interaction with technology especially in a constantly changing technological world. The developing, designing, programming and psychological techniques learnt from my experience at Carnegie Mellon University will go forth to impact and change the world. I have always known that technology is a big part of my life. My goals to work with technology; creatively, helping others understand how to interact with technology, engage with one another designing tools and programming interfaces that are user-friendly cannot be attained without proper training, instruction and practice.
