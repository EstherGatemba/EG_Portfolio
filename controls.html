Sauna Control

The goal of this project was to understand the role of feedfoward, feedback, and affordance in controld. The solution that we, as designers, needed to tackle was how to give clues of communication to users on the expected interactions with designs. With a control, the user should be able to know how to interact with the tool of control, and to understand what to expect when the control is triggered.
